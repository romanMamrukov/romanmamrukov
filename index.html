About


I am Romans Mamrukovs, a Technical Specialist with a passion for solving technical challenges and providing top-notch support to both the public and business sectors. With a background in software and hardware support, networking, security, and a strong foundation in IT operations, I am well-equipped to address a wide range of technical issues. My journey in the world of IT has spanned several roles and diverse experiences, shaping me into a versatile and adaptable professional. In my spare I do websites, 3D graphics and games, software projects, work on various business ideas and help others to improve theirs.


Work Experience


- At Kin+Carta, I served as a Match Day Support Executive and 2nd Line Support Specialist. I played a pivotal role in ensuring the seamless execution of EFL events, offering technical support, maintaining EFL websites, and optimizing service delivery for EFL-related activities.


- During my work at Recast, I worked as a Technical Support Operative, contributing to the resolution of technical challenges. I also played a vital role in training and enhancing the TechOps department, ultimately improving the Recast fan experience.


- At BuildReFix, where I've been since October 2018, I serve as a Lead Technician. Here, I provide a wide array of technical services, including software and hardware support, systems setups, network management, security, and more. I also contribute to making technical guidelines and documentation, as well as providing IT skills training.


- As an IT Support Technician at SimplyFixIT, I excelled in delivering exceptional customer technical support, resolving issues across various channels, and assisting clients in selecting the right repair service or devices.
